ParaZero Technologies (NASDAQ:PRZO), an aerospace defense company developing autonomous solutions for the global manned and unmanned aerial systems industry, announced it has received a new purchase order from an Israeli defense entity for its DefendAir counter-unmanned aerial systems platform. The order includes delivery of an evaluation kit featuring DefendAir net pods along with live exercises and training intended to support rapid operational deployment and readiness. The DefendAir system uses patented non-explosive net-launcher technology designed to neutralize hostile drones while minimizing collateral impact and addressing evolving aerial threats across urban and battlefield environments.
